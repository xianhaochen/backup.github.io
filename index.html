Xianhao Chen

Assistant Professor, The University of Hong Kong
Email: xchen [at] eee.hku.hk

I am an assistant professor at the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, the University of Hong Kong, leading the Wireless Information & Intelligence (WILL) lab. I obtained my Ph.D. degree in electrical and computer engineering from the University of Florida in 2022, supervised by Prof. Yuguang Fang, and my B.Eng. degree from Southwest Jiaotong University, Chengdu, China, in 2017, supervised by Prof. Gang Liu. My research interests include wireless networks, edge computing, distributed learning, and network security.

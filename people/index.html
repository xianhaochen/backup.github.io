WILL Lab

The Wireless Information & Intelligence (WILL) lab focuses on AI-native next-generation wireless networks, covering the topics on 5G/6G systems, edge computing, distributed machine learning, and network security. Our long-term vision is to design next-generation wireless networks to support ubiquitous intelligence with superior performance.
